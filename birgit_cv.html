Professional Achievements

I am a qualified clinical neuropsychologist with a wide range of skills and experience.

I have expert knowledge in neuropsychological assessment and rehabilitation of patients with stroke, brain injury, and other neurological conditions associated with neuropsychological or psychiatric disturbances.

Based on extensive clinical experience and research, I have developed specialist expertise in the assessment and management of mild brain trauma and functional neurological disorders.

I have a particular clinical and research interest in highly complex conditions, including chronic mild brain injury and trauma-related disorders. This expertise is based on my work within acute and community neuro-rehabilitation services and mental health teams at Dorset Healthcare University Foundation Trust.

I am highly skilled in neuropsychological, cognitive and language assessments and produce well-evaluated, evidence-based clinical reports.

My rehabilitation approaches include memory training, information-processing training, Cognitive Behavioural Therapy, Schema-focused Therapy, Systemic Family Therapy, Clinical Hypnosis and Client-Centred Therapy.

I have a strong interest in clinical and applied neuropsychological research. My evidence-based approach has contributed to service improvements and audits. I have presented nationally and internationally and have produced over 40 scientific papers, two books and a psychological test.

I am clinical lead of the Acquired Brain Injury Rehabilitation and Adult Neuropsychology Services in Dorset. I provide training to Stroke, Brain Injury, Neurology, Older Adult and Mental Health teams and contribute extensively to clinical psychology and neuropsychology training programmes at Bournemouth, Southampton and Exeter Universities.

In 2012, I was invited by the International Headache Society in Copenhagen to provide recommendations for psychological management programmes for posttraumatic headache.

In 2025, I organised the Brain Injury conference “Holistic Brain Injury Rehabilitation that works”, showcasing biopsychosocial rehabilitation approaches developed by the services I lead.

Since 2002, I have also maintained a private practice, undertaking expert witness work predominantly in personal injury claims and medical negligence cases. I have also worked with family courts and prison services.
