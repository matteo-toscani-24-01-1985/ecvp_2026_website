Sponsors

Confirmed Sponsors

The organizers are grateful for the support of the following sponsors:

Rank Prize

Rank Prize supports talented researchers with grants and studentships, focusing on funding in the fields of nutrition and optoelectronics – two disciplines upon which the founder, Lord Rank, built his businesses.

Perception by SAGE

Perception and its open-access sister journal i-Perception, by SAGE Publishing, have been supporting the ECVP for years.

SAGE is a global academic publisher of books, journals, and a growing suite of library products and services. SAGE focuses on publishing impactful research and enabling robust research methodology.

PeerJ

PeerJ Publishing has been sponsoring the ECVP since 2021, and are proud to continue offering PeerJ Awards at ECVP 2025. There will be a PeerJ Award winner for Best Poster every day of ECVP. Each award comes with a prize of a free publication in any of PeerJ’s high-quality Gold Open Access journals and a feature about the winner’s research on the PeerJ blog. Best of luck to all poster presenters!

The Applied Vision Association (AVA)

To celebrate Tom Troscianko’s contribution to the field of vision science and to reflect his wide range of interests, the Tom Troscianko Award funds attendance at ECVP, while encouraging innovative science and interesting travel. It is a pleasure to announce that once again the The Applied Vision Association (AVA) and ECVP committee will be collaborating to offer the Tom Troscianko award to adventurous early-career scientists attending ECVP 2025 in Mainz.

Bulbitech

Bulbitech provides automated functional eye examinations generating biomarkers with eye-tracking technology.
Our BulbiCAM device features Pupillometry with RAPD assessment, Eye Movement Perimetry, Acuity, Contrast Sensitivity, Smooth Pursuit, Saccade tasks, Nystagmography, and more.
Visit our booth to discover how our technology can support the early detection of ophthalmological and neurological disorders.

Tobii

Tobii is the global leader in eye tracking, with over 20 years of experience providing valuable insights into human behavior and cognitive processes. Our versatile technology is used across a wide range of research fields as a non-invasive, objective, and reliable measure of oculomotor and cognitive functions. Tobii’s solutions have been featured in 15,000 publications over the past decade, including 1,447 in 2024 alone.

Johannes Gutenberg University Mainz (JGU)

Johannes Gutenberg University Mainz (JGU) is a prominent public research university in Germany, home to about 30,000 students and 5,000 scientists from over 120 countries. The university emphasizes interdisciplinary research, academic excellence, and international collaboration, offering a wide range of disciplines to foster innovation and global responsibility. We thank JGU for the outstanding support of ECVP 2025!

Friends of Johannes Gutenberg University Mainz (Freunde der Universität Mainz e.V.)

Since its founding in 1951, the association Friends of Johannes Gutenberg University Mainz (Freunde der Universität Mainz e.V.) has fostered a strong and lively relationship between the university and members of the public, Gutenberg alumni, institutions, and businesses. With around 900 members, it is the largest non-profit association supporting Johannes Gutenberg University Mainz (JGU). The association provides funding for projects that cannot be covered, or only partially covered, by public resources. We sincerely thank the Friends of Johannes Gutenberg University Mainz for their kind support of the Spotlight in Vision keynote lecture!

Rhein-Main-Universitäten (RMU)

Together with the Goethe University Frankfurt and the Technical University Darmstadt, JGU Mainz forms a strong research and education network in the Rhine-Main University Alliance (RMU). As part of the RMU, we benefit from close cooperation, diverse study programs, and outstanding academic networks in one of Germany’s most dynamic regions. We are grateful for the support and opportunities provided by the RMU that strengthen our research and teaching efforts.

Pupil Labs

At Pupil Labs, we build cutting-edge eye tracking tools. Our latest system, Neon, is modular and adapts to your research. It works in any environment – from labs to mountains, VR to classrooms. It handles all types of subjects. It integrates easily with tools like LSL, Unity3D, PsychoPy and MATLAB. Powered by deep learning, Neon delivers robust gaze, pupil and eyelid data. Backed by 10+ years in research, we’re here to support your scientific goals.
